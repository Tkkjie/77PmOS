# 模板：YouTube 官方 — 標準回覆

## 使用場景

回覆 YouTube 官方團隊的各類信件（邀請、合作項目、確認事項等）。

## 模板

---

主旨：Re: {原始主旨}

Hi {對方名稱},

Thank you for reaching out!

{回覆內容，根據來信類型調整：}

{如果是邀請：We're interested in this opportunity and would like to learn more about the details.}

{如果是確認事項：We've reviewed the information and here's our response: ...}

{如果是政策更新：Thank you for the update. We've noted the changes and will adjust accordingly.}

Please let us know if you need anything else from our end.

Best regards,
{署名}

---

## 備註

- YouTube 官方信件通常用英文溝通
- 語氣要正式專業
- 注意辨別真偽（檢查寄件人網域）
